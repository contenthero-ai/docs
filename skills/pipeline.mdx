`contenthero-pipeline` is the hero skill. It takes a post from research to publication, and it is where ContentHero's grounding becomes a draft that sounds like you.

## The five phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ground">
    Pull your top outliers and mine the few best for hook, structure, and pacing. Read your brand kit for voice. Read your own top posts. Search the knowledge base for your stance on the topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draft">
    Your agent synthesizes a hook and caption that emulate your proven patterns, in your voice, and presents the draft for your approval. See [Voice synthesis](/skills/voice-synthesis).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Produce">
    Generate the media (often an image chained into a video), grounded in your visual style, via the [generate skill](/skills/generate).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assemble">
    Create the post with the approved caption, attach the media, and configure each platform destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ship">
    Schedule it, or publish it now, after you confirm destinations and timing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The line that defines it

Your agent drafts the copy, grounded in your real context (your outliers, brand voice, and past posts), and you approve it before anything ships. The skill supplies the grounding and the method; you keep the final say. Nothing schedules or publishes without your explicit approval.
